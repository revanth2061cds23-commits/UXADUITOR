### **Sync Screen**
Sync Screen bridges the gap between your user flows and Figma. It automates the tedious chore of capturing, organizing, and drawing mobile screenshots for UX audits and flow mapping. 

Using our companion **Web Uploader**, you can drag and drop your screenshots, visually mark interaction tap hotspots directly on the images, and sync them to Figma instantly.

### **💡 HOW TO USE:**

1. **OPEN THE WEB UPLOADER:**  
   Go to the link above: `https://revanth2061cds23-commits.github.io/UXADUITOR/web-app/`
2. **REGISTER / LOG IN:**  
   Create an account or sign in.  
   *(⚠️ **Important:** You must log in with the **same account credentials** in both this Figma plugin and the Web Uploader so they connect.)*
3. **UPLOAD SCREENSHOTS:**  
   Enter a Flow Name, then drag and drop your screenshots (or click to browse) into the left upload box.
4. **SET TAP HOTSPOTS (Optional):**  
   Click anywhere on the preview images on the right to place a red "tap hotspot" indicating where the user interacted with the screen. 
5. **REARRANGE & SYNC:**  
   Use the arrow buttons to arrange screens in chronological order, then click **"Sync Flow to Figma"**.
6. **IMPORT TO FIGMA:**  
   Open the **Sync Screen** plugin in Figma, log in with the same account, select your flow, and click **Import**. The plugin will automatically layout the chronological frames, visual tap hotspots, and connecting user flow arrows on your canvas in seconds!
